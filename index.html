WHAT IS MAGIWORD?

So, you started learning new langage. You mastered grammatics by doing tons of exersices, you practiced your speech chating with native speakers. But there is just one thing you can't retain - your vocabulary. You try to remember the words using flip cards, reminding them from your notebook, but the words just won't retain in your mind. Or maybe sometimes you feel too lazy to even open some translator page. If the foregoing is true about you, MagiWord might come in handy!


In the XIX century thre was a German psycholigist called Hermann Ebbinghaus who stydied the way our brain works. In his book "Memory: A Contribution to Experimental Psychology" he described the nature of forgetting things. Long story short, Ebbinghaus came up with the hypothesis that reminding words at the right time could prevent us from forgetting them and can help us remember words spending less time and making less effort learning them. The forgetting curve on the left side vividly describes this concept.


Why am I telling you this? That's because MagiWord's algorithms are based on Ebbinghaus' forgetting curve! That's right, no more irrational cramming, all you need is to keep adding words into your vocabulary and check your messages time after time to remind the words that you added. You can also thanslate any text on the webpage and add it to your vocabulary in a pop-up window.
